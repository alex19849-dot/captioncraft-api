About PostPoet

Built for resellers who want better listings, faster.

PostPoet was created to solve a simple problem, writing listings is repetitive, slow, and most AI tools write like they're trying far too hard.

You know the type, awkward buzzwords, fake enthusiasm, and descriptions that sound like a budget fashion catalogue from 2009. Nobody needs that nonsense when listing a jumper on Vinted.

What PostPoet Does

PostPoet helps you generate clean, useful resale listings for platforms like Vinted and eBay.

Add item details manually, speak them using voice input, or upload photos, front, back, labels, flaws, close-ups, whatever helps tell the story of the item, and PostPoet turns that into polished listing copy that actually sounds human.

Built for Real Sellers

Whether you're clearing your wardrobe, flipping charity shop finds, running a resale side hustle, or listing stock daily, PostPoet is designed to save time without making your listings sound robotic.

Clear titles, natural descriptions, relevant hashtags, and no cringe wording. Just useful copy that helps buyers understand what you're selling.

Always Improving

PostPoet is actively being refined, with smarter photo reading, stronger listing detail recognition, better platform tuning, and sharper output quality being improved over time.

The goal is simple, make listing online quicker, easier and better looking, without the waffle.

Simple in, solid listing out.
