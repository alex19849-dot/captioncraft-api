PostPoet FAQ

How does PostPoet work?
You describe what’s happening in the photo, choose a tone + length, and PostPoet generates 5 unique captions that match that vibe.

Can I use PostPoet for selling products?
Yes. Use the “Product selling direct” tone for subtle conversion focused captions that stay PG, benefit led, and creator friendly.

Does PostPoet support all content types?
Yes. PostPoet works for lifestyle creators, influencers, small sellers, resellers, brands, micro businesses, and product drops.

Is there a voice input option?
Yes. You can hold the mic icon and speak your description instead of typing. This is ideal for anyone who struggles writing text or prefers quick voice flow creation.

Are generations limited?
You get 10 free per day on the free plan. The Pro plan removes limits.

Is the content safe to post?
Yes. PostPoet stays PG-13 and avoids explicit sexual content, hate speech or abusive text.
