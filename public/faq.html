PostPoet FAQ

How does PostPoet work?

You describe your photo or product, choose a tone and length, and PostPoet generates 5 unique captions based on your input.

Can I use it for selling products?

Yes. The Product Selling Direct tone is built specifically for creators, sellers and resellers who want natural, benefit led captions.

Does it work for all niches?

Yes. PostPoet works for lifestyle content, resellers, influencers, creators, micro businesses and product drops.

Does PostPoet support voice input?

Yes. Hold the mic icon to speak your description and let the generator convert it.

How many free captions do I get?

The free plan includes 5 free generations per day. Pro removes limits.

Is the content safe?

Yes. All captions are PG-13 and avoid explicit, hateful or abusive content.

Do you store captions or images?

No. Captions and images are processed in real time and not saved.
