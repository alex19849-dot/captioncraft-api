PostPoet FAQ

Quick answers about using PostPoet for Vinted and eBay listings.

What does PostPoet do?

PostPoet helps sellers create clear Vinted and eBay listings from item details or uploaded photos.

Can I upload photos?

Yes. Pro users can upload up to 6 photos per listing, such as front, back, label, flaws or close-up details.

Does it write Vinted titles and descriptions?

Yes. For Vinted, PostPoet creates a title, description, item details and relevant hashtags.

Does it write eBay listings?

Yes. For eBay, PostPoet creates a keyword-rich title and clear item description without hashtags.

Will it make things up?

PostPoet is designed to avoid guessing brand, size, condition, measurements or flaws unless those details are provided or clearly visible.

How many free listings do I get?

The free plan includes 5 free generations per day. Pro removes the daily limit.

Does voice input work?

Yes. You can use the mic button to speak your item details instead of typing them.

Do you store my photos or listings?

No. Photos and listing text are processed in real time and are not saved by PostPoet.
